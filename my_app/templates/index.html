About Us

Services

We value your efforts to get a better soceity

What our Volunteers do?

Volunteers play a major role in this Garbage management system. Usually volunteers apply for a part-time or full-time job, and get hired by others (except home) and they are allocated their convenient area to collect garbage from houses,shops etc, or any other work according to the job they apply and get paid for it.

What our Shop Keepers do?

Shopkeepers play a very significant role, they assign volunteers for each home and street and collect the garbage regularly. And pay the volunteer accordingly for the amount of garbage collected and later the sell the garbage to garbagecollector and get paid for it.

What our Garbage collectors do?

Garbage Collector Role is way similar to Shopkeepers role, for every garbagecollector there will approve some Shopkeepers under them in their area. And on regular bases they collect the garbage from their Shopkeepers through volunteers and pay them, later on they get paid by the compaines who are willing to buy garbage and recycle them.
